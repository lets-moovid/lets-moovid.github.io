Welcome!

My research focus is on mathematical modeling of the spread of infectious disease in human populations.

In particular, I am interested in the evaluation of vaccinations on the transmission process and the epidemiological and health economic impact of different vaccination strategies. So far, I have worked on Influenza, COVID-19, Invasive Meningococcal Disease, HPV and Syphilis.
