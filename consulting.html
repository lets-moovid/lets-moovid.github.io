Workshops

If you and/or your team would like to deepen your knowledge about a specific topic, a workshop can help you achieve this. Please contact me, if you have a specific request.

Topics of previous workshops include:

  • Modeling in epidemiology and health economics (Decision Trees, Markov Models, Dynamic Transmission Models, Agent-Based Models)
  • Calibration methods for Infectious Disease Models
  • Vaccine Recommendations in Germany (STIKO, G-BA, role of modeling, etc.)
  • Health Economic Evaluations (cost-effectiveness, cost-utility, cost-of-illness, etc.)
